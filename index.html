Sinopsis

Rick Sanchez is an example of a typical "mad scientist". He is a genius, but he is irresponsible, alcoholic, selfish, a little depressive and with little sanity. Rick for various reasons ends up moving into his daughter Beth's house and at that moment he meets his grandson, Morty, a 14 year old boy, shy and not very smart. At with his grandson, Rick and Morty live a variety of adventures throughout the cosmos and universe and parallel universes. And it is through so many experiences and reflections that Rick tries to make sure that his grandson Morty doesn't end up like his father, Jerry, a very unsuccessful man who, despite his good intentions, turns out to be quite useless on many occasions and is very and depends a lot on his wife, Beth, Rick's daughter. Despite being very close, Rick and his grandson experience moments in which Summer, Morty's sister, occasionally joins in the colorful adventures brought on by Rick.
